hw-03: Practicing D3

For the first visualizaiton, we extracted the data from the Tokyo 2020 Olympics Games Medals Rank and tried to see the distribution of gold, siliver, and bronze medals for the top five countries. Grouped bar chart would be a appropriate graph to describe this kind of data since each countries has three measures. Then, we could group three measures for each country and use bar chart to visualize the measures. For the second visualization, we focused on the data of the Apple Stock prices in June 2015. We use x-axis to represent the datetime and y-axis to represent stock closed price. Thus, the location of each dot indicates the date or its corresponding stock closed price. Therefore, scatter plot would be a approriate visual tool.

Both of the datasets come from kaggle.com.You can access here :OlympicMedals AppleStocks
